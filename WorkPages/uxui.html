Bus Stop | Mobile App Design (2018)

This project was inspired by the needs of bus users. The idea behind this application was to give the users a tool where they can look for bus lines, see if there is a bus stop near their current location, track buses movement, and use the application as a payment control. The target for this app was students and professionals from 15 - 24 years old.

Health2Go | Mobile App Design (2018)

This project was inspired by the need for people who want to lose weight but cannot cook their own meals. It gives the user the possibility to choose a dish from the menu and observe the ingredients it contains. The user can also see the information of the person who will cook their dish, and track the food when it is on its way. It is an application designed for professionals who fail to follow diets because they do not have time to cook.
