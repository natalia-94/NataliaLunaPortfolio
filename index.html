This is me!

Natalia Luna is a Computer Systems Engineer and Multimedia Creator based in Obregon, Sonora. She studied Computer Systems Engineering at the Instituto Tecnologico Superior de Cajeme (ITESCA) specialising in Multimedia Production. She continued her preparation with courses about Web Development, video production, 3D Animation and Stop Motion animation, and different programming languages like C#. She is now pursuing a Specialization in Virtual Reality by Goldsmiths University of London. She has worked for local artists as their video producer and video editor, and is currently working as a Software developer at Iron Mountain.
Natalia also spends time practicing and sometimes teaching dance, an activity that has helped to shape her disciplined character and to believe that with effort and perseverance it is possible to achieve her goals.
